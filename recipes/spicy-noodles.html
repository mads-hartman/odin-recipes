Pad See Ew

Description

This delicious Thai noodle dish is often called "Drunken Noodles," because they are maybe the best possible thing you could eat on a night out.

Ingredients

  • Wide rice noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • chili paste
  • Red pepper
  • Snow peas
  • Stir-fry oil
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Broccoli
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water. Let sit at least 30 minutes.
  2. Mince garlic and chop vegetables.
  3. Sautee garlic and chili paste.
  4. Add vegetables to pan.
  5. Add soy sauce and more oil.
  6. Add noodles, cook until soft.
  7. Garnish with peanuts and any additional toppings or sauces.
  8. Enjoy!
