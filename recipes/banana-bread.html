Banana Bread

Description

This bread is a banana's highest potential.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Over-ripe bananas
  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips
  • Baking soda
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mash bananas.
  2. Melt butter.
  3. Whisk egg.
  4. Add all ingredient into mixing bowl, stir well.
  5. Grease bread pan and pour in batter.
  6. bake 45 min at 350 degrees.
  7. Remove from pan and allow to cool for 20min before slicing.
  8. Enjoy!
