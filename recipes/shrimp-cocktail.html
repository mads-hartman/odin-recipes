Shrimp Cocktail

Description

A delicious summertime food. Perfect for an appetizer or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Small shrimp, cooked and peeled
  • Ketchup
  • Celery
  • Cilantro
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lime juice
  • Tortilla chips
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Mix vegetables, shrimp, and tomato broducts together in a large bowl.
  3. Add cilantro, salt, pepper, and lime juice to taste
  4. Set in fridge to chill.
  5. Enjoy!
